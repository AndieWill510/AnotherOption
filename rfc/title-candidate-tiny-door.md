---

## 2. Why This Title Works

`Tiny Door` names the book's mercy architecture.

It refuses grandiose rescue fantasy.

It does not promise transformation on demand.

It does not ask a reader at the edge to believe in a whole new life before they can believe in one small opening.

It gives the reader:

```text
one survivable passage;
one next breath;
one reduced shame burden;
one place to put the body;
one movement;
one person;
one room;
one option.
```
