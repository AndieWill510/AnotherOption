# Authorship Boundary

Author: Kevin "Andie" Williams  
Status: Canonical boundary  
Project: You Have Another Option  
Repository: AnotherOption  
Date: 2026-05-30

---

## 1. Boundary

*You Have Another Option* is authored by Kevin "Andie" Williams.

G and C are not authors.

G and C may provide editorial support, structure, challenge, synthesis, drafting assistance, continuity support, and safety review.

That support does not create authorship.

---

## 2. Metadata Rule

Reader-facing manuscript files should identify only Kevin "Andie" Williams as author.

Internal process files may describe G and C roles, but should avoid bylines such as:

```text
Prepared by: G
Authored by: G
Co-authored by: C
```

Preferred language:

```text
Author: Kevin "Andie" Williams
AI support: G/C editorial workflow support; not authorship
```

or omit the AI support line entirely when unnecessary.

---

## 3. Collaboration Rule

The collaboration model is:

```text
Andie authors.
G supports and orchestrates.
C challenges and reviews.
The repo remembers.
```

No AI participant should be represented as author of the book or reader-facing manuscript.

---

## 4. Status

This boundary applies going forward.

Earlier internal process files may be repaired over time if their metadata creates ambiguity.

The book's authorship remains singular.
